About me

I received the B.S. degree from the Southwest Jiaotong University of Engineering Mechanics, in 2018, and the M.S. degree from Zhejiang University, in 2021. I am currently a PhD candidate in the Department of Building Environment and Energy Engineering, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Hong Kong, China. My main research interests include Isogeometric Analysis, Topology Optimization and Additive Manufacturing.

Research
